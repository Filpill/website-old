3D Printed Lithophane Lamp

Overview

Project Summary:

The goal of this project is create a 3D printed lamp with the main feature of having replaceable lithophane pictures of a standardised dimension.

The lithophane stl's are generated using the following website: https://itslitho.com and printed with and Ender 3 pro.

The pictures can be slotted into the frame after unscrewing and replacing the lid of the lamp.

The light socket is secured into the assemblies back plate in conjunction with a frontal attachment to constrain the foward movement of the light socket.

Currently I have two slightly different designs to accomadate either an E14 lamp socket or an E27 lamp socket.

The version shown below in the pictures is the E14 litho lamp and the total estimated print time is 26 hours.
